We Build Where People Gather

SKHB Construction Ltd is a London-based construction company specialising in building and maintaining beautiful restaurant spaces. From complete builds to ongoing maintenance, we handle every detail under one roof.

From a complete kitchen fit-out to the final coat of paint, we manage everything ourselves — electrics, plumbing, tiling, joinery, the lot. No subcontractor roulette. One team, one standard.

Founded by Sununu, SKHB Construction is a hands-on team of specialists who have spent years learning what makes hospitality spaces work. Every member of our crew understands the unique demands of restaurant environments — from gas-safe kitchens to front-of-house finishes that need to survive thousands of covers.

We listen first, plan precisely, and deliver exceptional workmanship within your budget and timeline. Our goal is simple: get you open and trading as quickly as possible, without cutting a single corner.

We're not the biggest. We're the ones who get it right.
