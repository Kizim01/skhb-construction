Where Design Meets Workflow

A great bar is not just a counter with taps behind it. It is a carefully engineered workspace where every centimetre matters: the height of the rail, the position of the ice well, the reach to the back bar, the angle of the drip tray. Get it right and your bartenders move faster, serve more, and waste less. Get it wrong and you feel it every single service.

At SKHB, we build bars that work as hard as the people behind them. We have constructed everything from intimate wine bars to high-volume cocktail bars and branded pop-up activations. Each one starts with the same question: how does your team need to work? From there, we design the counter, the back bar, the shelving, the lighting, the plumbing, and the electrics as one integrated system.

Our joinery team builds custom bar counters, back bar units, bottle displays, and shelving to your exact specification. Copper fronts, reclaimed timber, marble tops, brass rails; whatever the brief demands, we fabricate it in-house. That means the finish is consistent, the tolerances are tight, and the character of the piece is exactly what you envisioned.

We also handle every supporting trade: plumbing for beer lines and waste, electrics for refrigeration and lighting, tiling for splashbacks, and flooring for the bar well. One team, one programme, one handover date. No coordination headaches, no finger-pointing between contractors.

What's Included

  • Bar layout design in consultation with your team
  • Custom bar counter fabrication and installation
  • Back bar construction: shelving, displays, bottle storage
  • Beer line and draught system plumbing
  • Refrigeration positioning and connection
  • Bar well drainage and ice well installation
  • Electrical installation: lighting, refrigeration, POS points
  • Decorative finishes: copper, brass, timber, tile, marble
  • Foot rail, speed rail and accessory installation
  • Splashback tiling
  • Under-bar storage and shelving
  • Final sign-off and snagging
